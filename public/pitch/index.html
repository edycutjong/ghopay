The Problem

Crypto payroll is fully public.

"When a DAO paid 47 contributors on-chain, 12 received targeted phishing emails within 24 hours — each referencing the exact salary amount visible on the explorer."

  • Employees are doxxed by their wallet balances
  • Compensation details are exposed to competitors
  • Treasuries face friction adopting on-chain rails

The Solution

Ghopay uses Stealth Addresses to mask individual salaries.

🕵️‍♂️

Invisible Transfers

Per-employee amounts are completely hidden on block explorers.

🔍

Auditable Compliance

HR uses Viewing Keys to generate compliance reports for auditors.

How It Works


Treasury Wallet
      │
      ▼  executeStealthBatch()
 Cloak SDK ──► unique stealth address per employee
      │
      ├──► Alice  →  stealth_addr_A  (5,000 USDC hidden)
      ├──► Bob    →  stealth_addr_B  (4,500 USDC hidden)
      └──► Charlie→  stealth_addr_C  (3,800 USDC hidden)

HR / Auditor
      │
      ▼  generateViewingKey()
 cloak_vk_... ──► decrypts payroll for compliance
