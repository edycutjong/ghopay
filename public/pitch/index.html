The Problem

Public crypto payroll is fundamentally broken

Targeted Phishing

When a DAO pays 47 contributors on-chain, 12 receive targeted phishing emails referencing the exact salary amount visible on the explorer.

Identity Exposure

On-chain analysis links wallets to real identities, compromising contributor safety and negotiating leverage.

Compliance Friction

DAOs need transparent treasuries, but individuals need financial privacy. Both shouldn't be mutually exclusive.

2 / 8

The Solution

Ghopay + Cloak SDK = stealth batch transfers

Execute batch payroll in a single click. Every employee receives funds in a unique, untraceable stealth address. Auditable for compliance via Viewing Keys.

Why Cloak Privacy SDK

The gold standard for Solana privacy

Ghopay leverages the Cloak SDK's UTXO shielded pool architecture for maximum privacy without losing auditability.

Stealth Addresses

Every payout generates a fresh `Keypair.generate()` destination. Wallet linkage becomes cryptographically impossible.

Viewing Keys

HR and Auditors can view the full payroll via `getNkFromUtxoPrivateKey()` — ensuring regulatory compliance without public explorer leakage.
